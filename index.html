Instructions

Cowabunga Dudes! The evil Shredder will give you a totally random number at the beginning of each round.
Each time you click on one of the gnarly Ninja Turtles, a specific amount of points will be added to your total score.
You can beat Shredder by matching your total score to his number. You will lose the game if your total score goes above Shedder's number.
The value of each totally tubular Ninja Turtle is hidden from you until you click on one of them.
Each time this wicked game starts, the values of each Ninja Turtle will radically change.
